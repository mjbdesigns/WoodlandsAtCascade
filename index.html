Not Just Apartments.
A Community.

Most apartment communities are designed around parking lots.

Woodlands at Cascade is designed around people. Nestled among mature trees and natural green spaces, Woodlands offers residents a quieter, healthier, and more connected lifestyle while remaining minutes from downtown Atlanta, shopping, dining, and major employers.

Meet The Team

Building More Than Apartments

At Capital City Properties, we believe great communities begin with care. Working alongside our partners at Star Group, our focus is simple: create places where residents feel safe, welcome, connected, and proud to call home.

"The highest form of luxury is care."

This philosophy guides everything we do — from thoughtful property improvements to community-building initiatives. We are committed to enhancing both the places we invest in and the lives of the people who live there.

Woodlands at Cascade is not simply being renovated. It is being reimagined as a nature-centered community where every improvement is made with the resident experience in mind.
